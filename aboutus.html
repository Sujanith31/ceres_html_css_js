About Ceres Agros

Ceres Agros is India’s leading manufacturer of 100% water-soluble granular fertilizers, developed using the groundbreaking Microphase Enrichment Technology (MET). Our science-driven solutions are tailored to meet the evolving demands of modern agriculture — ensuring higher efficiency, better yields, and sustainable soil health. We are not just a fertilizer company. We are a vision, a movement, and a mission — one rooted in precision, inspired by performance, and dedicated to progress.

Our Technology: MET (Microphase Enrichment Technology)

What is MET?

MET is an advanced, layered nutrient delivery system. Every Ceres Agros granule is manufactured with multi-phase nutrient compartments, allowing for targeted, time-sensitive release of macro and micronutrients such as:

  • Nitrogen (N) – stabilized, non-volatile forms
  • Phosphorus (P) – non-DAP phosphorus compounds
  • Potassium (K) – neutral pH potassium
  • Micronutrients – Zn, Fe, Mn, B, Mg, Mo in chelated or protected forms
  • Organic Core – fortified with carbon molecules and bio-enhancers to stimulate root interaction and nutrient absorption

How MET works?

  • Precision layering: Nutrients are embedded in concentric shells
  • Controlled release: Each layer dissolves in response to soil moisture and root interaction
  • Zero waste: Nutrients are released only when the plant needs them — avoiding leaching and fixation
  • Uniform impact: Every granule contains a full profile, ensuring even plant nutrition

Why It Matters:

  • No urea, no DAP – just pure, plant-friendly compounds
  • Works across diverse soil types and climates
  • Reduces application frequency and farmer input costs
  • Promotes balanced plant growth, root health, and improved yield quality

Quality in Every Trait

At Ceres Agros, we define high yield by quality, not just quantity. A true harvest is measured by size, weight, shape, colour, taste, aroma, and shelf life — and our technology ensures every crop meets these seven essential traits.

Nourishing the Soil, Scientifically

Our formulations work from the ground up, improving soil porosity, carbon levels, pH balance, and boosting beneficial microflora. This enhances water retention, builds natural disease resistance, and creates a fertile, well-structured soil ready for cropping with slow-release nutrition and root activators.

Phase-Specific Plant Care

We deliver balanced nutrition tailored to each plant phase, from rooting to fruiting. Our understanding of bio-chemical triggers helps us customize inputs that drive efficient growth at every stage — especially during flowering and fruit development.

Unlocking Plant Potential

Ceres Agros solutions also build stress tolerance and improve nutrient uptake efficiency, ensuring faster and complete bio-reactions. The result: healthier plants, stronger yields, and consistent growth throughout the crop cycle.
